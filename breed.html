Havanese Breed Overview 🐾

The Havanese is a small but lively companion dog known for its silky coat, affectionate personality, and playful spirit. Originally from Cuba, this cheerful breed thrives on human connection and makes a wonderful family companion. Below is a quick overview of key characteristics of the Havanese breed.

Category Details
Origin Cuba
Group Toy Group
Average Height 8.5–11.5 inches
Average Weight 7–13 pounds
Coat Type Long, silky, soft, and wavy
Temperament Affectionate, intelligent, playful, social
Exercise Needs Moderate daily activity
Good for Families? Yes — excellent companion dog
